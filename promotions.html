“Fall into Reading” Book Sale
Period: October 1 - October 15, 2024
Venue: Main Library Lobby

Embrace the cozy season with our “Fall into Reading” Book Sale! From October 1st to 15th, head to the Main Library Lobby and enjoy fantastic discounts on a wide selection of books. Whether you’re looking for fiction, non-fiction, or children’s literature, you’ll find great deals and hidden gems. All proceeds support library programs and services. Don’t miss out on the perfect opportunity to stock up on your fall reading list and support your community library!

“New Member Welcome” Special
Period: November 1 - November 30, 2024
Venue: Main Library and Online

New to Silent Library? We’re rolling out the red carpet with our “New Member Welcome” Special! Sign up for a library membership between November 1st and 30th, and enjoy exclusive benefits, including a free tote bag, a 10% discount at our library bookstore, and access to special member-only events. Join us in-person at the Main Library or online to start your membership journey. Discover all the resources and community programs available to you, and make the most of your new library experience!
