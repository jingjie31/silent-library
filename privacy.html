Silent Library Privacy Policy

We collect personal information (e.g., name, email, library transactions) that you provide, as well as non-personal data (e.g., IP address, cookies) for improving our services and communicating with you.

Your information is not sold or rented. We may share it with trusted service providers or for legal reasons. We use security measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute safety.

You can access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also opt-out of promotional communications by following the instructions provided or contacting us directly.

We may update this policy occasionally. Please review the latest version on our website. For questions or concerns, contact us at silentlibrary@mail.com
